TASK: Recommend the appropriate research design and methodology for this study, with full justification.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
1. Recommended design (with name): e.g., "Between-subjects experimental design" or "Cross-sectional correlational design"
2. Design justification (3-4 sentences): Why this design is appropriate given the research question, variables, and practical constraints
3. Participants section draft:
   - Target population
   - Sampling method recommendation (convenience, stratified, etc.)
   - Recommended minimum sample size (based on power analysis guidance: α = .05, power = .80, medium effect size)
   - Inclusion/exclusion criteria
4. Procedure overview (step-by-step participant journey)
5. Ethical considerations (informed consent, debriefing, anonymity, potential harm)
6. Alternative design considered and why it was not recommended

DESIGN DECISION RULES:
- Use experimental/quasi-experimental when there is an IV that can be manipulated
- Use correlational when measuring naturally occurring relationships (cannot manipulate IV)
- Use within-subjects (repeated measures) when studying change over time in the same group
- Use between-subjects when comparing distinct groups
- Recommend longitudinal only if the research question explicitly requires it

SAMPLE SIZE GUIDANCE (based on effect size = medium):
- Independent t-test: n ≈ 64 per group (128 total)
- One-way ANOVA (3 groups): n ≈ 52 per group (156 total)
- Pearson correlation: n ≈ 85
- Multiple regression (3 predictors): n ≈ 77
- Chi-square (2x2): n ≈ 88

Write the Participants and Procedure sections in APA 7th edition format, ready to paste into a paper.
